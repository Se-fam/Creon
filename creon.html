About Créon

A studio of
one — for now.

Créon is a design studio built around making. Not just designing things on screens, but actually producing them — with hands, tools, and a real interest in craft.

Right now it's just me, with a burning passion for creating things and a growing set of tools to do it with. Stickr! is the first product, but the vision is bigger: a place where good design feels accessible, tangible, and genuinely useful.

Because good design should feel like it belongs in your life.
